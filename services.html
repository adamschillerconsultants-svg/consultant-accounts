How I Can Help

A complete billing administration service tailored to the needs of self-employed medical consultants.

I take care of every stage of the billing cycle on your behalf — from raising the initial invoice through to collecting payment — so you can focus on delivering excellent patient care without the administrative burden.

Invoice Preparation & Dispatch

I prepare accurate invoices on your behalf and send them promptly to patients and insurers, maintaining a professional image for your practice at every touchpoint.

Insurance Liaison

I communicate directly with insurance companies to submit claims, follow up on outstanding payments, and resolve any queries — ensuring optimum remuneration for your services and saving you hours of administrative back-and-forth.

Patient Payment Chasing

When patients have outstanding balances or excess/co-payment shortfalls, I handle follow-up correspondence professionally and tactfully on your behalf.

Payment Tracking & Reconciliation

I maintain a clear, up-to-date record of all invoices raised and payments received, so you always have a complete picture of your outstanding accounts.
