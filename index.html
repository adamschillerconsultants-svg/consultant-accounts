How I Can Help

From preparing your invoices to chasing overdue payments, I take care of the complete billing cycle on your behalf.

Invoice Preparation & Dispatch

I prepare accurate invoices on your behalf and send them promptly to patients and insurers, maintaining a professional image for your practice at every point.

Insurance Liaison

I communicate directly with insurance companies to submit claims, follow up on outstanding payments, and resolve any patient queries — ensuring optimum remuneration for your services and saving you hours of administrative back-and-forth.

Patient Payment Chasing

When patients have outstanding balances or excess/co-payment shortfalls, I handle follow-up correspondence professionally and tactfully on your behalf.

Payment Tracking & Reconciliation

I maintain a clear, up-to-date record of all invoices raised and payments received, so you always have a complete picture of your outstanding accounts.
